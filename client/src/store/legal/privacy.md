# Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal information SonSoul (Gabriel Elyth Gomez)
collects and how we use it.

## What we collect

- **Account & order data:** your email address, name (if provided), password (stored
  only as a secure hash), order history, and download activity.
- **Payment data:** processed by **PayPal**. We receive a confirmation and a
  transaction/capture ID; we **do not receive or store your card or bank details**.
- **Technical data:** a truncated IP address and basic device/browser information for
  security, fraud prevention, and analytics.

## How we use it

- To deliver your products, provide your download library, and send receipts and
  account emails (verification, password reset, order confirmation).
- To operate, secure, and improve the store.
- To send occasional updates **only if you opt in** to marketing.

## Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service
providers that run the store: **PayPal** (payments) and **Resend** (transactional
email), and where required by law.

## Cookies & storage

We use minimal local storage for your cart, session token, and to remember that you
dismissed the install prompt. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

## Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data by
emailing **gabrielelythgomez@gmail.com**. You can unsubscribe from marketing at any
time. Some records (e.g., completed orders) may be retained where required for legal
or tax purposes.

## Data security

Passwords are hashed (bcrypt), sessions use signed tokens, and master files are
stored outside the public web root and delivered only through expiring, single-use
links.

## Children

The store is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their
data.

## International users

Your data may be processed in the country where our servers and providers operate.
By using the store you consent to this transfer.
