# Refund Policy

SonSoul sells **digital products** — beats, instrumentals, licenses, and sample packs
— that are delivered immediately by download and cannot be returned.

## All sales are final

Because a digital product cannot be "given back" once it has been delivered, **all
sales are final and non-refundable**. By completing your purchase you acknowledge
that you receive immediate access to the product and you **waive any right of
withdrawal or cancellation** once the download link has been issued.

## Exceptions

We will consider a refund or replacement only in these cases:

- **You were charged more than once** for the same order (duplicate charge).
- **The files are corrupt, empty, or the wrong product** and we are unable to
  provide a working replacement.
- **The download links never worked** and we cannot resolve the issue.

To request help under an exception, email **gabrielelythgomez@gmail.com** within
**7 days** of purchase with your order number. Approved refunds are issued to the
original PayPal payment method.

## Not grounds for a refund

- Changing your mind after downloading.
- Buying the wrong license tier (contact us — we can often upgrade/credit instead).
- Lack of the software or knowledge to use the files.
- Downloading after the link expired due to delay on your side (we can usually
  re-issue a link as a courtesy).

## Exclusive licenses

Because an Exclusive License removes the beat from sale permanently, exclusive
purchases are **strictly non-refundable** once delivered.
